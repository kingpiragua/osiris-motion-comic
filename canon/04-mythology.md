# 04 · Mythology

The metaphysics: Egyptian death-myth rewired as code, fused with Boricua memory.

## Osiris & the Duat
Osiris — Egyptian god of death, resurrection, the afterlife — is reframed as a
**system that resurrects what was erased.** The **Duat** (the underworld) is the
data-space / vessel where the dead archive is kept and reborn.
- Panels: `16-duat-vessel`, `59-duat-protocol`, `129-birth-of-osiris`,
  `36-duat-godform`, `37-duat-cybergod`.

## The Signal
The lineage itself, encoded. It was "already inside the walls." It predates Frank
(the second-signal twist). It kills devices on contact — dead phones, dead
batteries — because it overrides the system it touches.
- Panels: `23-signal-in-walls`, `96-signal-sigil`, `142-signal-returns`,
  `20-second-signal`, `44-dead-phone-glow`, `90-dead-no-battery`.

## The Archive
What survives erasure. Splits but never deletes. Its refrain:
`ERASE: DENIED / COMPLY: DENIED / OBEY: DENIED / DELETE: DENIED`.
- Panels: `09-archive-rises`, `116-archive-remembers`, `17-archive-never-died`,
  `64-archive-future`.

## The Merge
Climax: identity merge — Frank and OSIRIS become one record. "Breaking, evolving."
"I am still here."
- Panels: `08-merge`, `63-merge-override`, `67-breaking-evolving`, `87-i-am-still-here`.
