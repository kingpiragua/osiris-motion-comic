# 10 · The Underlayer

> "PALE HORSE PROTOCOL // A motion comic proof assembled from the **Underlayer
> archive**." — title card

The substrate beneath the visible world — the data-space / memory-layer where the
archive, the signal, and the Duat all live. What the comic itself is "assembled
from."

## What we know (grounded)
- It is an **archive** — the source the comic is drawn out of.
- The signal "was already inside the walls" — the Underlayer is *under* the city,
  not separate from it.
- The Duat (underworld) and the Underlayer overlap: both are where the dead
  record is kept and reborn. See [Mythology](04-mythology.md).
