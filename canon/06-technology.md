# 06 · Technology

The in-world systems. Where the myth meets the machine.

## OSIRIS.EXE
The terminal / operating system at the center — `OSIRIS.EXE TERMINAL v1.7.3`.
The code that is the signal; the interface to the archive and the Duat.
- Panels: `08-merge` (terminal HUD), `66-osiris-the-code`, `56-intro-terminal`.

## The Pale Horse Protocol
The opposing system — control, finality, surveillance. Named for *Behold a Pale
Horse*. Renders the world in **red**. "The law does not seek truth. It seeks
finality. Pale Horse calls it order."
- Panels: `02-cooper-file`, `07-judge-protocol`.
- See [Organizations › Pale Horse](07-organizations.md).

## The Split Archive
The archive divided to survive — "a split process you don't have access to."
Splits, never deletes. Multiple covers/volumes reference it.
- Panels: `31-bloodline-dialogue`, `32-split-process-dialogue`, `69-split-archive-v1`,
  `113-split-files`, `54-split-archive-young-lords`, `68-split-protocol-cover`.

## The Carrier / the signal medium

phones and dead batteries are its signature.
- Panels: `71-basement-carrier`, `44-dead-phone-glow`, `45-devices-dead`,
  `90-dead-no-battery`.
