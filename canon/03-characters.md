## Frank / Disk Darián (kingpiragua)
The boy from 1987, Humboldt Park / St. Sylvester. Hip-hop, the blackbook, the
artist's desk. His line runs from child → artist → the merge with OSIRIS.
- *"Cooper died for writing it down. Frank died for living it. Now the record refuses to burn."* (`08-merge`)
- Also the real-world author/creator credit (Disk Darián).
- Key panels: `03-school`, `18-diskdarian-blackbook`, `72-diskdarian-nitano`,
